# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our Standard

We want this repository to be a serious, generous place to build open creative software. Be direct, respectful, specific, and useful. Debate ideas hard; treat people well.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Give feedback that helps the work improve.
- Credit inspiration, references, and contributions.
- Respect different levels of experience and context.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, discrimination, sexualized language or imagery, and sustained disruption of project work.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this standard. Serious or repeated violations may lead to a ban from project spaces.

To report a concern, open a private security advisory or contact the maintainers through the repository owner profile. Reports will be reviewed with care and handled proportionately.
